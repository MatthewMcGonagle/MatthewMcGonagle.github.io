Here is a collection of reading material that I find interesting. I have made an effort to include as many legally, freely available material as possible.

Textbooks

  • Hastie, Tibshirani, and Friedman. Elements of Statistical Learning: Data Mining, Inference, and Prediction. URL: https://web.stanford.edu/~hastie/ElemStatLearn
  • James, Witten, Hastie, and Tibshirani. An Introduction to Statistical Learning: with Applications in R. URL: https://www-bcf.usc.edu/~gareth/ISL/ .
  • MacKay. Information Theory, Inference, and Learning Algorithms. URL: https://www.inference.org.uk/itprnn/book.html .
  • Blum, Hopcroft, and Kannan. Foundations of Data Science https://www.cs.cornell.edu/jeh/book.pdf .
  • Bishop. Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning .
  • Shalev-Shwartz and Ben-David. Understanding Machine Learning: From Theory to Algorithms. URL: https://www.cs.huji.ac.il/~shais/UnderstandingMachineLearning/understanding-machine-learning-theory-algorithms.pdf.
  • Boyd and Vandenberghe. Convex Optimization . URL: https://web.stanford.edu/~boyd/cvxbook/ .
  • Rota and Baclawski. An Introduction to Probability and Random Processes. URL: https://archive.org/details/GianCarlo_Rota_and_Kenneth_Baclawski__An_Introduction_to_Probability_and_Random_Processes.
  • Vershynin. High-Dimensional Probability: An Introduction with Applications in Data Science. URL: https://www.math.uci.edu/~rvershyn/papers/HDP-book/HDP-book.html
  • Wasserman. All of Statistics: A Concise Course in Statistical Inference.
  • Evans. Introduction to Mathematical Optimal Control Theory. URL: https://archive.org/details/Lawrence_Craig_Evans__An_Introduction_to_Mathematical_Optimal_Control_Theory.
  • Evans. An Introduction to Stochastic Differential Equations . URL: http://citeseerx.ist.psu.edu/viewdoc/download?doi=10.1.1.299.5323&rep=rep1&type=pdf.
  • Boyd. Chebyshev and Fourier Spectral Methods .
  • Berger. A Panoramic View of Riemannian Geometry. http://citeseerx.ist.psu.edu/viewdoc/download?doi=10.1.1.384.4746&rep=rep1&type=pdf .
  • Colding and Minicozzi. A Course in Minimal Surfaces .

Programming

  • Drepper. What Every Programmer Should Know About Memory .

Mathematical Surveys

  • Riviere. Exploring the Unknown: the Work of Louis Nirenberg on Partial Differential Equations. URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1505.04930

    In addition to discussing the work of Louis Nirenberg, this survey contains a lot of information on the history of PDE and topics in PDE.

  • Kazdan. Applications of Partial Differential Equations to Problems in Geometry . URL: https://www.math.upenn.edu/~kazdan/japan/japan.pdf

    Gives a brief and condensed account of PDE in geometry.

  • Chen, Bang-Yen. Riemannian Submanifolds: A Survey . URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1307.1875 .
  • Chen, Bang-Yen. Riemannian Geometry of Lagrangian Submanifolds. URL: https://projecteuclid.org/download/pdf_1/euclid.twjm/1500574989 .
  • Richey. The Evolution of Markov Chain Monte Carlo Methods URL: http://citeseerx.ist.psu.edu/viewdoc/download?doi=10.1.1.295.4478&rep=rep1&type=pdf .
  • Cabre. Elliptic PDEs in Probability and Geometry. Symmetry and Regularity of Solutions . URL: http://citeseerx.ist.psu.edu/viewdoc/summary?doi=10.1.1.154.1881 .
  • Vazquez. The Mathematical Theories of Diffusion. Nonlinear and Fractional Diffusion . URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.08241.
  • Schonert. A Walking Tour of Microlocal Analysis . URL: http://www.math.uchicago.edu/~may/VIGRE/VIGRE2006/PAPERS/Schonert.pdf
